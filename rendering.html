Artist Statement

When designing this I didn't know where I was going with it until I opened up Blender and started piecing together what this would look like. I started thinking of ghosts and very surreal themes that also can be seen through the lenses of loneliness, ghosts wandering within their own world with nothing but shape and color that separates them. I also saw this as a message within human identity and even with different variations of one another we still wander the earth all the same.
