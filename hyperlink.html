Artist Statement

This piece was something that came to me immediately when writing the code itself, I first started with the title and I felt like I could make it as if it were speaking to the audience itself, a map into the mind of the medium. It became pretty clear that I wasn’t speaking for the website itself but for myself, an interpretation of being locked behind a screen and what it means to feel true isolation when you have this grand creative expression you can implore into your work. I see myself in this piece because I see how even with word and expression you still can feel unheard, as if others won’t understand you as hard as you try to push that message out through a piece because it will never truly encapsulate what it means to be you, only a fragments of it.
