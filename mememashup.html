Artist Statement

In making this piece I focused on what me and my brother have laughed at in the past and composed it in a sort of a love letter to what drives our humor and what we love about meme culture. When composing this I looked for what fit together like a puzzle, the explosion, the goofy voicing, the more nerdy aesthetic that still incorporates modern meme culture. This assignment helped dive into my humorous side and let me express my comedic preferences in the style of modern meme culture.
